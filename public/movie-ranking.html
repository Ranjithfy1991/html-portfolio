The Best Movies According to RajithKumar K

My top 5 movies of all-time.

LOVE GENER MOVIES

  1. 96 Movie

    Two high school sweethearts meet at a reunion after 22 years and reminisce about their past.

  2. Vaaranam Aayiram

    A father and son help each other through growing up, romance, tragedy, and adventure..

  3. Alai Payuthey

    After a difficult romance in a class-divided society, the love birds find out marriage is not what they thought it would be.

  4. Bombay

    A Hindu man and a Muslim woman fall in love in a small village and move to Mumbai, where they have two children. However, growing religious tensions and erupting riots threaten to tear the family apart.

  5. Siva Manasula Sakthi

    The movie is about the Blossom of love, fun and lots more between the characters Siva and Sakthi. The 1st half shows the blossom of love in the mind of Siva while we find a new turn during the second half where Sakthi reciprocates it.

ACTION GENER MOVIES

  1. Baashha

    An auto driver, Manikam, desperately tries to hide his dark underworld side to keep his promise to his father.

  2. Ayan

    A young man's smuggling lifestyle takes a serious turn, when a fierce rival tries to eliminate him from the business.

  3. Sandakozhi

    Balu, on a visit to his friend's hometown, falls for the latter's sister, Hema. When he learns that this area is ruled by a local goon, Kaasi, Balu teaches him a lesson. Now, Kaasi is seeking revenge.

  4. Aalavandhan

    Nandu and Vijay are brothers, who were abused by their stepmother during their younger days. Nandhu, who is mentally ill, decides to kill Vijay's fiancee as he visualizes her as his stepmother.

  5. Padaiyappa

    The death of Padaiyappa's father destroys his family. But his luck changes, and he is able to lead a prosperous life, until his nemesis plots to ruin his happiness once more.

CRIME DRMA GENER MOVIES

  1. Vada Chennai

    A young carrom player in north Chennai becomes a reluctant participant in a war between two warring gangsters.

  2. Thegidi

    A plainclothe officer falls in the hands of phony agents ,which sets off repercussion ending in series of a massacre events.

  3. Thupparivaalan

    A detective is hungry for a challenging case to investigate and the mystery of a murdered dog leads to a rival who could be the match for him.

  4. Vikram Vedha

    Vikram, a no-nonsense police officer, accompanied by Simon, his partner, is on the hunt to capture Vedha, a smuggler and a murderer. Vedha tries to change Vikram's life, which leads to a conflict.

  5. Asuran

    The teenage son of a farmer from an underprivileged caste kills a rich, upper caste landlord. How the pacifist farmer saves his hot-blooded son is the rest of the story.

THRILLER GENER MOVIES

  1. Maharaja

    A barber seeks vengeance after his home is burglarized, cryptically telling police his "lakshmi" has been taken, leaving them uncertain if it's a person or object. His quest to recover the elusive "lakshmi" unfolds.

  2. Theeran Adhigaram Ondru

    Theeran, a police officer, is entrusted with the task to solve the mystery behind a series of murders. The only clue left behind is that all the murders are committed in the same pattern.

  3. Thani Oruvan

    Siddharth Abimanyu, an influential scientist, is involved in various illegal medical practices. Mithran, an efficient IPS officer, decides to expose him.

  4. Raatchasan

    A sub-inspector sets out in pursuit of a mysterious serial killer who targets teen school girls and murders them brutally.

  5. Papanasam

    Desperate measures are taken by a man who tries to save his family from the dark side of the law after they commit an unexpected crime.

SCI-FI GENER MOVIES

  1. Maanaadu

    On the day of a public conference by the state's Chief Minister, his bodyguard and a police officer are stuck in a time loop.

  2. Enthiran

    A brilliant scientist creates a humanoid robot to protect mankind, but things go awry when human emotions are programmed and inner turmoil causes the robot to switch sides.

  3. Indru Netru Naalai

    Elango and his friend Pulivetti come into possession of a time machine and they start profiting from it.However,their meddling with time prevents the death of a gangster, who begins to wreak havoc in their lives.

  4. Tik Tik Tik

    After discovering that an asteroid the size of a city is going to impact Earth in less than a month, India recruits a misfit team to save the country.

  5. Miruthan

    A traffic police officer teams up with his friend and doctors in order to escape a deadly zombie apocalypse in the town and find out the possible antidote to stop it before time runs out.

HORROR GENER MOVIES

  1. Yaavarum Nalam

    A man experiences a supernatural occurrence at his newly purchased apartment; a soap opera being telecast exclusively on his TV, which shows the future of his family.

  2. Aval

    A young married couple begin to encounter several paranormal attacks, when a family moves into a neighbouring house, that is being haunted by a vengeful spirit, which leads them to perform an exorcism in order to help save the neighbours.

  3. Demonte Colony

    After consuming liquor, four friends decide to do something interesting and visit a haunted bungalow. However, they do not realize that they have returned home with an evil spirit.

  4. Pizza

    A pizza delivery boy lands in a mysterious circumstance and it works a dramatic change in his life.

  5. Pisasu

    A violinist experiences paranormal experience in his flat after a death of a girl who he tries to save due to accident.Later he realises that actually the spirit is helping him and he tries to find out who killed the girl by accident.
